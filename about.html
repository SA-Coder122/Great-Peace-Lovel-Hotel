OUR STORY

Welcome to Great Peace & Love Hotel

Nestled in the heart of Ashalaja, Greater Accra Region, Great Peace & Love Hotel stands as a beacon of comfort, tranquility, and exceptional hospitality. Our 10-bedroom boutique hotel has earned a stellar reputation as one of the most trusted and highly-rated accommodations in the Ashalaja-Amasaman area.

With a remarkable 9.3/10 rating, we pride ourselves on creating an environment where serenity meets luxury. Whether you're a business traveler seeking a peaceful retreat or a leisure guest looking for a relaxing getaway, our hotel offers the perfect blend of modern amenities and warm, personalized service.

Our mission is simple: to provide every guest with an unforgettable experience characterized by comfort, privacy, and genuine Ghanaian hospitality. From our spacious, well-appointed rooms to our attentive staff, every detail is crafted to ensure your stay is nothing short of exceptional.
