FREQUENTLY ASKED

Common Questions

What is the check-in and check-out time?

We offer 24-hour check-in service for your convenience. Check-out is flexible and can be arranged based on your needs. Please contact us to make special arrangements.

How can I book a room?

The easiest way to book is via WhatsApp at +233 534 078 670. You can also call us directly at the same number. Our staff is available 24/7 to assist with your booking.

Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes, we can arrange airport transfers upon request. Please contact us in advance to make arrangements and discuss pricing.

Is parking available at the hotel?

Yes, we provide free private parking on-site for all our guests. Your vehicle will be safe and easily accessible throughout your stay.

Do you provide meals and is breakfast included?

We have an on-site restaurant serving breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Breakfast availability can be confirmed during booking. We offer flexible meal support including early breakfast and late-night meals.

Can you arrange tours to tourist destinations?

Absolutely! We organize tours to over 20 tourist destinations across Ghana. Contact us to discuss your interests and we'll help plan an unforgettable experience.
